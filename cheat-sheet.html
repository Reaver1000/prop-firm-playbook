Trailing Drawdown Trap

  • Drawdown TRAILS highest balance. Never resets down.
  • $50K peak $51,200 → floor $48,700
  • Drop to $49,000 = $300 from failure
  • Updates at 4:59:59 PM ET
  • Flat by 4 PM. Every day.

30% Consistency Rule

  • Applies at payout request, NOT during evaluation
  • No single day > 50% of total profit since last payout (30% legacy)
  • +$1,500 best day → need $3,000 total (50%) or $5,000 (30%)
  • Rule gone after 6th approved payout
  • Spread profit across days before requesting payout

Daily Loss Limit (DLL)

  • Most NT strategies have zero DLL enforcement
  • Does NOT fail evaluation — resets next day
  • $50K example: 2 × $400 losses = $800. Next trade = near limit.
  • Halt trading when within $100 of limit
  • Only applies to EOD accounts; intraday eval has no DLL

Pre-Trade Checklist — Read Before Every Session

  • Account above drawdown floor? CHECK
  • DLL not hit today? CHECK
  • Position size within max micros? CHECK
  • Stop set BEFORE entry? CHECK
  • Profit target at 1.5x–2.0x stop? CHECK
  • No overnight positions planned? CHECK
  • Daily PnL within DLL safety margin? CHECK
  • Flat by 4:59 PM ET confirmed? CHECK

Contract Sizing

  • MNQ: 1 tick = $0.50. 1 point = $2.
  • MES: 1 tick = $1.25. 1 point = $5.
  • ES: 1 tick = $12.50. 1 point = $50.
  • Formula: qty = floor(Risk$ / (StopTicks × TickValue))
  • ALWAYS clamp to account max.

Emergency Protocols

  • Breach imminent: Flat everything. Walk away.
  • DLL hit: No more trades today. Period.
  • Drawdown floor approached: Reduce size 50% or stop.
  • Big winning day: Check consistency % before next trade.
  • 3 losses in a row: Mandatory 15-min break.
