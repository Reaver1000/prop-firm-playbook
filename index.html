You're Not Failing Because of Your Strategy

My ORB strategy had an 88.6% simulated pass rate. I still failed 3 evaluations. The problem wasn't the strategy. It was the rules I didn't understand.

The Trailing Drawdown Trap

The drawdown is NOT a fixed number. It trails your highest balance. On a $50K account, peak at $51,500 and the floor moves to $49,500. Give back $1,000 — you're at $50,500, still up $500. One more $600 loss and you're at $49,900. Breached. Despite being down only $100 from where you started.

The 30% Consistency Rule

Applies at payout request, NOT during evaluation. No single day can exceed 50% of total profit since last payout (30% legacy). A $1,500 best day means you need $3,000 total before requesting payout. Rule gone after 6th approved payout.

The DLL Blind Spot

Most NinjaTrader strategies have zero Daily Loss Limit enforcement. On a $50K account, two $400 losses ($800) and you're one trade away from the $1,000 DLL. It does NOT fail your evaluation — it resets tomorrow. But hit it today and you're done trading until 5 PM ET.

Contract Sizing Disqualification

My MNQ strategy calculated 250 contracts on a tight stop. Apex $25K max: 40 micros. First trade = instant disqualification. Most traders never check this.
