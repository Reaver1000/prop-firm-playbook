You're Not Failing Because of Your Strategy

My ORB strategy had an 88.6% simulated pass rate. I still failed 3 evaluations. The problem wasn't the strategy. It was the rules I didn't understand.

The Trailing Drawdown Trap

The drawdown is NOT a fixed number. It trails your highest balance. A trader who peaks at $51,200 on a $50K account can fail at $49,000 — despite being up $1,900 overall.

The 30% Consistency Rule

One big winning day can fail your entire evaluation. A +$900 day that is 80% of total profit = automatic failure. Even profitable traders get caught.

The DLL Blind Spot

Most NinjaTrader strategies have zero Daily Loss Limit enforcement. Three $400 losses on a $50K account ($1,200) and one more trade breaches the $1,250 DLL.

Contract Sizing Disqualification

My MNQ strategy calculated 250 contracts on a tight stop. Apex $25K max: 40 micros. First trade = instant disqualification. Most traders never check this.
